Julius Caesar

He came. He saw. He conquered.

Mildly interesting facts about Julius Caesar:

  • I - He named the month July after himself.
  • II - Caesar is not his family name, but a cognomen that means 'Hairy'. His legacy was so strong that future rulers modeled their title after him. Kaiser and Tsar are a few examples.
  • III - He was kidnapped by pirates, swearing to them that if he was freed he would exact his revenge. Years later, freed, he did.
  • IV - He had a son with Cleopatra, called Caesarion.
  • V - He died, betrayed by his loyal retainers, stabbed to death in a conspiracy.
